University of Minnesota Twin Cities(UMN) was my first university in America after I graduated from Whatcom Community college. It located in the edge of the cities Minneapolis and Saint Paul in Minnesota state. The Mississippi River divided the school into two parts, which was east bank and west bank. We usually say east bank in Saint Paul, and west bank in Minneapolis. Most of the buildings located on the east bank; however, Carlson School of Management located on the west bank, which was the building I took account and economics classes. I would like to say I began my real university life in UMN. However, because of the extremely bad weather, my body health situation became worse, and I always had a fewer. All parents love their children! My parents advised me to transfer to a university which in a warm state; therefore, I came to California.
