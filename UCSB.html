I was admitted to UCSB Fall 2017 on March, but I actually were in Class 2018 since I just spent my senior year there. It is hard for me to describe my life in UCSB. Most of the people think UCSB is a party school, and actually it is. In Isla Vista, they drink all the night from Monday to Sunday. Probably next week they will continue. But that is not my life! I would like to say library was my Second home, and I sometime believe I stayed longer in library than in my apartment. Academic science life is like that! I had no words wo complain since the moment I chose Mathematics as my major.



It is hard to summary my university life because it is so complex. I was in Whatcom Community College in Washington State for my freshman and sophomore year, University of Minnesota Twin Cities for my Junior year and University of California Santa Barbara for my Senior year. Life was enjoyable in WCC, relax in UMN and nervous in UCSB. I grew up quickly in my last year at university, and I feel like I understand the world and life deeply. After I graduate, I have stayed in America for five years, and I decided to change an environment for myself.
