Whatcom Community College(WCC) is the first college I attended when I came to America. At that time, I was about sixteen years old. Thanks to the running start program, most of the colleges in Washington State do not require students to provide their senior school diplomas. I really did not know what my future would be like in China since I had no high school to go, so my parents sent me to America. My dream started here!


I got both of my honor degree for Associate of Arts and Science and High School diploma in 2016, and I studied in WCC for two years. I was also admitted by University of Minnesota Twin Cities at that time. Before I came to WCC, I was just a normal Chinese girl like everyone who confuse the next step of life. However, I found my interesting of mathematics right now, and I began my next step.
